Recognizing Problem Gambling

It's important to recognize the signs of problem gambling. Ask yourself these questions:

  • Do you spend more money or time gambling than you can afford?
  • Do you gamble to escape from problems, worries, or boredom?
  • Do you feel a need to bet increasing amounts of money to get the same thrill?
  • Have you tried to stop gambling but been unsuccessful?
  • Do you lie to hide the extent of your gambling?
  • Do you neglect your family, friends, or work because of gambling?
  • Have you borrowed money or sold possessions to gamble?
  • Do you feel anxious, guilty, or depressed after gambling?

If you answered yes to several of these questions, you might be experiencing a gambling problem.
